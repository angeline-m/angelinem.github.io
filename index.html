About

Hello, World! I am a second-year Computer Software Development student for NAIT's Digital Media & IT program. I grew up with the Internet by my side. I am passionate about visual media in the digital world, and my artistic passions have led me to carving new roles and responsibilities at places of employment to enhance the digital presence of these companies. Being able to marry my creative interests with programming has been novel and exciting.

My computer-related interests include graphic design, typography, web design & development, video editing, video game modding, pixel art, and using spreadsheets to organize my life. My non-computer related interests include puzzles, escape rooms, singing, dancing, and tutoring.
