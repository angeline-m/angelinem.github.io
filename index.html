About

Hello, World! My name is Angeline and I'm a web developer. I truly believe I am destined for a career in digital media and development. The first sign was when I designed a personal homepage on MS Paint at 8 years old.

These days, I love creating beautiful web applications using my programming skills and keen eye for design. My applications are constructed with user experience in mind. I consider design elements that programmers might overlook such as site flow, brand colors, contrast, and typography.

I'm a proud ambivert. I like working independently but also enjoy helping others and excel at teamwork. I've been lucky enough to lead and coordinate for many teams including a capstone project. My interests include video games, puzzles, trail hiking, singing, and dancing.
